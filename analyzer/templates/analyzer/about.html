Project Overview

Engineering Career Analyzer is a comprehensive web application designed to help students and professionals make informed decisions about their engineering careers.

The platform provides real-time data analysis, interactive visualizations, and personalized recommendations for six major engineering branches. With data-driven insights, users can compare placement rates, salary packages, future trends, and required skills across different engineering disciplines.

Mission

Empower students with data-driven career insights

Vision

Become the go-to platform for engineering career guidance

Purpose & Objectives

Career Guidance

Help students choose the right engineering branch based on interests, placement data, and future trends.

Market Analysis

Provide real-time insights into placement rates, salary trends, and industry demands across branches.

Skill Development

Recommend relevant courses, projects, and skills to help users prepare for their chosen careers.
